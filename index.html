Open Data Incubator for Europe

Open Data Start ups

We are pleased to announce the launch of an Open Data Incubator for Europe. The Incubator stands to support entrepreneurs, startups and small to medium sized enterprises building new products and services based on open data to create economic and social value in Europe.

Join us!

If you are an early start-up around open data, have an idea to set one up, or are a small business that wants to explore an open data opportunity, please leave your email here and we will keep you informed about the first European incubator for Open Data businesses.
